Wtana

Who Are We ?

We are an initiative affiliated with the Ministry of Youth and Sports and UNICEF that works to integrate expatriates into Egyptian society, solve the problem of racism to which they are exposed, and help them adapt in all ways without any distinction between expatriates and Egyptians.

Why us

There are 9 million expatriates in Egypt. Circumstances forced them to immigrate to us from all over the Arab world. Therefore, we had to include them among us and coexist with us despite different customs and traditions and without any kind of prejudice or racism.
